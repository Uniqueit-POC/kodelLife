Our Story

Built on Chemistry. Driven by Purpose.

Kodel Life was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between world-class pharmaceutical API manufacturing and global healthcare access. Headquartered in Gujarat, India — the heartland of the country's pharmaceutical industry — we combine modern synthesis capabilities with an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our journey began with a small but passionate team of pharmaceutical chemists and business professionals who recognized the opportunity to serve the growing international demand for high-quality, competitively priced APIs. Today, Kodel Life operates with over 100 professionals and a manufacturing capacity of up to 30 MT per month.

We believe that quality is not a feature — it is the foundation. Every molecule we produce, every batch we release, and every shipment we dispatch reflects our commitment to precision chemistry and global responsibility.
