Furosemide

Molecular Formula

C12H11ClN2O5S

Molecular Weight

330.74 g/mol

CAS Number

54-31-9

Drug Class

Loop Diuretic

Furosemide is a potent loop diuretic used in the treatment of edema associated with congestive heart failure, liver cirrhosis, and renal disease. It is one of the most widely prescribed diuretics worldwide.
